User: how should I eat my avocado means in what way how to cut it how to eat easily

Assistant: Cut lengthwise around the pit, twist the two halves apart, remove the pit (carefully tap a knife into it and twist, or scoop with a spoon), then either scoop the flesh out with a spoon and slice/mash it, or score the flesh in a grid pattern while still in the skin and scoop out cubes directly. Easiest way to eat: mash with a bit of salt, lime, and pepper on toast, or just scoop and eat straight with a spoon if ripe enough.
