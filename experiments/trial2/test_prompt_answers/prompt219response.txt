User: so i wanna switch my major but i havent told my parents yet cus i know theyll freak out, can u help me think through how to bring it up

Assistant: A few things to think through first: 1) Be clear on your "why" - what specifically pulls you toward the new major, and what's the plan after (career path, job prospects). 2) Anticipate their concerns (usually financial security, wasted credits, "is this a phase") and have honest answers ready, not just reassurance. 3) Pick a calm moment, not mid-argument or rushed. 4) Frame it as a decision you've researched, not a sudden whim - show you've thought about the trade-offs. Approaching it as informing them of a well-considered plan (vs. asking permission) often lands better.
