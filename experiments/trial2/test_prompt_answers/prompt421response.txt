Assistant: A few key ones to start: 1) What is the difference between DELETE and TRUNCATE? (DELETE removes rows with rollback/WHERE support and logs each row; TRUNCATE removes all rows instantly, minimal logging, can't use WHERE). 2) What is a PRIMARY KEY? (uniquely identifies each row, can't be NULL). 3) What is a JOIN? (combines rows from two tables based on a related column - INNER, LEFT, RIGHT, FULL variants). 4) What is normalization? (organizing data to reduce redundancy). 5) Difference between WHERE and HAVING? (WHERE filters rows before grouping, HAVING filters after GROUP BY). I can continue with the remaining 15 if you'd like - happy to expand on JOINs, indexes, constraints, subqueries, and aggregate functions specifically.
