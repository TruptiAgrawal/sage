Assistant: Key C++ tools: `substr(pos, len)` extracts a substring. `find(str)` returns index of first occurrence or `string::npos`. `std::accumulate` for subarray sums. `std::max_element`/`min_element` for range max/min. Sliding window pattern (two pointers, expand/shrink based on a condition) handles most substring/subarray problems efficiently - use it when looking for longest/shortest substring meeting a condition. Prefix sum arrays help with subarray sum queries in O(1) after O(n) preprocessing. Two-pointer technique works well for sorted-array subarray problems. `unordered_map` tracks character/element frequency for substring uniqueness problems.
