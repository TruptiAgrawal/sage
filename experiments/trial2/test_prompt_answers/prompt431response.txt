Assistant: Two bugs: 1) `scanf("%d", guess)` should be `scanf("%d", &guess)` - scanf needs the address of the variable, not its value, otherwise this causes undefined behavior/crash. 2) `if (guess = 7)` uses assignment (`=`) instead of comparison (`==`) - this assigns 7 to guess and always evaluates as true, rather than checking if guess equals 7. Corrected: `scanf("%d", &guess);` and `if (guess == 7)`.
