Assistant: Steps: 1) Document your concrete contributions/impact since your last review (numbers if possible). 2) Research market rate for your role to have a number in mind. 3) Ask for a dedicated meeting rather than bringing it up casually. 4) Open with your value: "I wanted to discuss my compensation given [specific achievements]. Based on my research, I believe [range] would be fair." 5) Be ready to listen and negotiate, not just present a demand.
