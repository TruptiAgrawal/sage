User: how should I make my Bangs look straight without using heat

Assistant: Try: 1) Wet-set them with a flat brush while blow-drying on cool setting (less heat damage than a straightener). 2) Use small velcro rollers overnight for a smooth, straight-ish finish. 3) Apply a light leave-in cream and comb straight down repeatedly while damp, letting them air-dry pinned flat with clips. 4) A silk/satin pillowcase reduces frizz overnight, keeping bangs smoother longer.
