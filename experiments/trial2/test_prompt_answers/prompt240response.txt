User: i think my teammate isnt pulling their weight on the group assignment but i dont wanna be the one who "snitches" to the prof, whats a better way to handle it

Assistant: Try addressing it with the teammate directly first: "Hey, I noticed you haven't gotten to [task] yet - is everything okay? We're a bit tight on time so wanted to check in." Give them a real chance to step up. If it continues, document contributions honestly (many profs ask for peer evaluations) rather than framing it as reporting - that's a normal, expected part of group work, not snitching.
