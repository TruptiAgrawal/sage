User: Best places to visit in Hyderabad for my Sunday outing only (10am-5pm)

Assistant: For a 10am-5pm Sunday outing: Start at Golconda Fort (10-11:30am, historic and scenic), then Charminar and Laad Bazaar for lunch and shopping (12-2pm), followed by Hussain Sagar Lake for a boat ride to the Buddha statue (2:30-4pm), and wrap up at Necklace Road for a relaxed evening walk before heading back by 5pm. This route keeps travel time reasonable given the timeframe.
