User: my partner's family invited me to something big and i havent met most of them yet, help me figure out how to not seem awkward

Assistant: A few tips: ask your partner beforehand about key people and any topics to avoid/bring up. Prepare a few easy go-to questions ("how do you know [host]?", "what's your favorite part of these gatherings?"). Stick near your partner early on for introductions, then branch out once comfortable. Being genuinely curious and asking questions usually works better than trying to have interesting things to say yourself.
