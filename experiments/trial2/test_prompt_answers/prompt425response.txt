User: Merit and demirits of writing my classwork with only black pen instead of blue

Assistant: Merits of black pen: prints/scans more clearly, often preferred/required for official documents, and appears more formal/professional. Demerits: some institutions specifically require blue for original signatures/handwritten answer sheets to distinguish originals from photocopies, and blue can be slightly easier on the eyes for long reading sessions. Check your institution's specific exam rules, since some explicitly mandate blue or black ink.
