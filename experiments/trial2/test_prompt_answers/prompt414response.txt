User: can u give me a dataset for sentiment analysis to train a model

Assistant: Popular sentiment analysis datasets: IMDb Movie Reviews (50k labeled reviews, binary sentiment), Twitter Sentiment140 (1.6M tweets, auto-labeled), Amazon Product Reviews (multi-domain, star-rating based), and Stanford SST (Sentiment Treebank, fine-grained sentiment). All are freely available on Kaggle or via Hugging Face's `datasets` library - `load_dataset("imdb")` is a quick starting point.
