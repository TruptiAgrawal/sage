User: What are the advantages and disadvantages of cloud computing? Explain IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS with examples.

Assistant: Advantages: cost efficiency (no upfront hardware), scalability on demand, accessibility from anywhere. Disadvantages: ongoing subscription costs, dependency on internet connectivity, potential security/compliance concerns with third-party data storage. IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service): provides raw computing resources like virtual machines and storage (e.g., AWS EC2) - you manage the OS and above. PaaS (Platform as a Service): provides a managed platform to build/deploy apps without managing infrastructure (e.g., Heroku, Google App Engine). SaaS (Software as a Service): fully managed software delivered over the internet (e.g., Gmail, Salesforce) - you just use the application.
