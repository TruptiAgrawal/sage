Assistant: As a tester, your Excel/Kanban tracker should include: Test Case ID, User Story linked, Test Scenario, Steps to Reproduce, Expected Result, Actual Result, Status (Pass/Fail/Blocked), Severity, Assigned Dev (if bug), and Retest Date. Your responsibilities: write test cases from user stories before development starts, execute functional/regression testing on completed features, log bugs with clear reproduction steps, verify bug fixes, and track defect trends over sprints (bugs found vs resolved) to report quality metrics to the Scrum Master. Your Kanban lane typically moves cards from "Ready for Test" → "Testing" → "Failed/Passed" → "Done".
