User: i wanna ask a professor to be a reference for grad school but i only took one class with them, is that weird to ask

Assistant: Not weird at all, as long as they can speak specifically to your work in that class. When you ask, remind them of specifics (what class, when, a project you did, your grade) and offer to send your resume/statement to help them write something concrete. If they seem unsure they can write a strong letter, that's a sign to ask someone else instead - but one class is plenty if the connection was meaningful.
